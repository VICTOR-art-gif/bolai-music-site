Bio

About
Bolai

Bolai Fang is a composer, sound designer, and new media artist studying Music for New Media and Music Education at the Peabody Institute of Johns Hopkins University.

His work focuses on hybrid scoring for games, film, and interactive media, combining orchestral writing, electronic production, rock and metal energy, and audiovisual design.

Alongside composition, he is interested in creative coding, game audio systems, music education, and building projects where sound, image, and interaction feel like one connected world.
